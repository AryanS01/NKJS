• NKJS is a Registered Society under the West Bengal Societies Registration Act, 1961
  • Established in 2011 under the leadership of noted philanthropist Mr. Yashwant Singh.
  • NKJS's prime objective is to create and support meaningful social activities through innovative solutions to address some of India's most pressing developmental challenges.
  • NKJS lays significant emphasis on leveraging the strength & availability of human resources taking the advantages of India's demographic dividend.
  • Following its model of direct social engagement, NKJS directly reaches out to the communities through trained manpower thereby creating the desired social impact.
  • NKJS's objective & vision is to display “Seva Bhav” by working & providing value added services in the field of Health, Education and Social sector ecosystems through collaboration, partnerships and by providing high-impact and scalable solutions.
  • NKLS works in alignment with the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals with the primary objective of improving “Good Health & Well-Being” (SDG Goal No. 3) & improving “Quality Education” (SDG Goal No. 4)
  • In the past 10 years, NKJS has positively impacted the lives of millions of Indians by focusing on the most marginalized groups and vulnerable population.
