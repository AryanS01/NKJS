Proposed Model - Mobile Medical Unit (MMU)
➤ Proposed project name - Jan Swasthya
➤ Services offered - Providing primary health care facilities to the local public
➤ Beneficiaries - General public living in the area
➤ Scope - Primary Healthcare facilities, Health Awareness
➤ Resources - Doctors, paramedic, drivers, other staff & social volunteers
➤ Data capture - Manual / Digital (as required)
➤ Schedule - Select service camp locations on select days
➤ Service Point - Local schools, Gram Panchayats, Anganwadi centres etc
➤ Referral - Local PHC / Health Institution
➤ Medicine Dispensation - Branded / General (as required and as per scope)
